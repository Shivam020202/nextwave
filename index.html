DEEP TMS FOR DEPRESSION

Restore Hope With Breakthrough Brain Stimulation

Major-depressive and treatment-resistant depression respond powerfully to Deep TMS. BrainsWay’s H-coil penetrates 4 cm into mood circuits, driving neuroplastic change without medication side-effects. In multi-centre trials up to 75 % of patients achieved remission or marked improvement after a 30-session course. Most feel lighter within two weeks, with zero downtime—so you can return to work, family and the activities you love while treatment is still in progress.

DEEP TMS FOR OCD

Quiet the Obsessions, Calm the Compulsions

FDA- and Health Canada-cleared for obsessive-compulsive disorder, Deep TMS targets the anterior cingulate and medial pre-frontal networks that drive intrusive thoughts and ritual behaviours. Our H7 helmet delivers focused pulses while you briefly think about your triggers; the circuit “re-learns” and the urge fades. Studies show a 38 % average reduction in Y-BOCS scores after six weeks, with continued gains at three-month follow-up. Relief—without systemic drugs.

Read More

DEEP TMS FOR SMOKING

Crush Nicotine Cravings — No Pills, No Patches

Deep TMS is the first non-invasive brain therapy cleared to help adult smokers quit. By modulating the insula and pre-frontal control centres, it dulls cue-induced cravings and boosts impulse control. Treatment lasts 18 sessions over three weeks; clinical trials reported 4× higher continuous quit rates than counselling alone. Pair it with our behavioural coaching and breathe freely—without weight gain or irritability.

DEEP TMS FOR ANXIETY

Reclaim Calm, One Pulse at a Time

Generalised anxiety, panic and social anxiety often stem from over-reactive limbic circuits. Deep TMS down-regulates these pathways while strengthening the cortical networks that keep worry in check. Patients commonly notice steadier sleep and fewer physical symptoms by session ten. Because the treatment is medication-free, there’s no sedation, cognitive fog or dependency—just a progressive return to calm confidence.

Read More

DEEP TMS FOR PTSD

Reset Your Brain’s Fight-or-Flight Circuit

Trauma locks the brain into hyper-alert mode. Our protocol targets the dorsolateral and medial pre-frontal cortex, helping them regain control over the amygdala’s alarm signals. Early studies in veterans show significant drops in CAPS-5 and PCL-5 scores, plus improved emotional regulation. Sessions are quiet, seated and last about 20 minutes, making Deep TMS a gentle yet potent complement to trauma-focused therapy.

Read More

DEEP TMS FOR ADDICTION

Break the Cycle—Strengthen Your Recovery

Whether alcohol, opioids, or stimulants, addiction hijacks the brain’s reward pathway. Deep TMS re-balances dopamine signalling and strengthens executive control, lowering relapse risk while you build new habits. Our evidence-based protocol can be started during early sobriety and paired with counselling or 12-step work. Patients report reduced cravings, better stress tolerance and a clearer path to long-term recovery.
